Precision Infusion System

At Q Medical Technology, we specialize in advanced drug delivery systems designed for the most critical patient needs. Our latest innovation is a smart infusion pump engineered to handle high-sensitivity medications that require perfect synchronization with a patient's cardiac rhythm.

Life-Saving Real-Time Response

In critical care, a delay of even a few milliseconds can be life-threatening. That is why our system utilizes a Real-Time Operating System (RTOS). This ensures the pump reacts instantly to cardiac fluctuations, delivering medication only when the heart rate is within a safe, normal range.

Safety & Security Features

  • Overdose Protection: The pump automatically halts delivery if the heart rate exceeds 3000 pulses, protecting the patient from dangerous complications.
  • Automatic Cessation: If no pulse is detected, the system stops immediately to ensure patient safety.
  • Emergency Protocol: Equipped with a physical emergency stop and wireless remote override capabilities.

Wireless Monitoring & Diagnostics

Our centralized monitoring system can control the device wirelessly for safety and testing:

  • System Controlled Wirelessly #1: A safety lockout initiated by the company. The system remains stopped until a doctor manually restarts it.
  • System Controlled Wirelessly #2: A remote diagnostic test. A green LED will flash to confirm all external components are successfully connected.
